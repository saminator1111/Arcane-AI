--ABOUT US--

Hello! We're the team behind Arcane AI! Or well for now, the solo developer behind Arcane Ai. My name is Arcane, and well this is my personal passion project.

I started working on Arcane AI early 2026 after being heavily inspired by Janitor ai, and the potential it had. I saw that with the right tools, the concept that was working already could be refined and expanded into something much bigger for its Open Ai side.

That's where I came in, I saw one day, after using the page for years with Open Ai API, that it coulnd't truely handle the level of customaztion and quality that I wanted to see in the bots I was making, and I wanted to make a platform that could.

That's when on a random Sunday afternoon, my day off from the dealership I was working at, I opened visual studio code and just played around with some concepts of the page. I had no real plan, and furthermore, I was an absolute beginner when it cam eot coding backend and API's.

But, I learned fast, and I had tones of help from some amazing people who felt my idea and concept was worth helping with, and from the, I start builiding the first page!


Any comments or sudjestions for what to add, or any bugs please feel free to send a message via our discord to the owner and we will review it as soon as we can! Thank you.
